DESCRIPTION

THIS IS A SIMULATION OF THE ACEY DUCEY CARD GAME. IN THE GAME, THE
DEALER (THE COMPUTER) DEALS TWO CARDS FACE UP. YOU HAVE AN OPTION TO
BET OR NOT TO BET DEPENDING ON WHETHER OR NOT YOU FEEL THE NEXT CARD
DEALT WILL HAVE A VALUE BETWEEN THE FIRST TWO.

YOUR INITIAL MONEY (Q) IS SET TO $100; YOU MAY ALTER THIS IF YOU WANT
TO START WITH MORE OR LESS THAN $100. THE GAME KEEPS GOING ON UNTIL
YOU LOSE ALL YOUR MONEY OR INTERRUPT THE PROGRAM.
